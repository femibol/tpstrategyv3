# Thumbnail Brief — Video #001

**Canvas size:** 1280 x 720 px (YouTube standard)
**Tool:** Canva (free or Pro)

---

## Variant A — "Rich vs Broke Split"

**Layout:** Vertical split down the middle

| Left side (green tint) | Right side (red tint) |
|---|---|
| Upward trending chart | Downward trending chart |
| Dollar signs / cash imagery | Empty wallet / bills imagery |

**Text overlay:**
- Top: **"5 HABITS"** (white, bold, large)
- Bottom: **"RICH vs BROKE"** (green "RICH" / red "BROKE")

**Font:** Bold sans-serif (Impact, Bebas Neue, or Montserrat Black)
**Background:** Dark/navy base with green and red color accents

---

## Variant B — "The Money Habits"

**Layout:** Center-focused

- Dark background
- Large gold/green dollar sign or money icon in center
- Bold white text: **"5 MONEY HABITS"**
- Subtext in yellow: **"that actually work"**
- Small upward arrow or chart icon near bottom

**Font:** Bebas Neue or Oswald Bold
**Colors:** Dark navy background, white + gold/green text

---

## Variant C — "Data-Backed" (authority play)

**Layout:** Left-aligned text with right-side visual

- **Left side:** Bold text stack:
  - "5 HABITS" (large, white)
  - "THE RICH" (green)
  - "USE DAILY" (white)
- **Right side:** Stock chart going up, or a clean bar graph visual

**Font:** Montserrat Black or Anton
**Colors:** Dark background, white text, green accent

---

## Design Principles (from playbook)

- Maximum 3-5 words visible at glance
- High contrast colors (dark bg + bright text)
- Green = money/growth, Red = warning, Gold = wealth
- No face needed — use financial imagery + bold typography
- Test at small size (mobile) — text must be readable at thumbnail scale
- Create all 3 in Canva, upload primary, A/B test with YouTube's native thumbnail testing
